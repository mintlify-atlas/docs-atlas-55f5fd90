## About this site

This knowledge base shares what I have learned and studied — from hands-on infrastructure engineering to software development best practices. Whether you're looking for step-by-step guides on Kubernetes, Docker, or Git, or quick troubleshooting tips for Linux and Python, you'll find real, practical content here.

I'm **Kar Chun Tan**, a Senior IT Engineer at AMD, certified Kubestronaut, and infrastructure & DevOps enthusiast. This site is my way of giving back to the developer community that helped shape my journey.
